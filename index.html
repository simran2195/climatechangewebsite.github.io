About Us

Climate Change Learning Lab

The ‘Climate Change Learning Labs’ in each of the states would facilitate knowledge exchange to relevant stakeholders, including students (school as well as college level), government officials from different departments, people representatives, vulnerable communities, NGOs, and media. These labs would enable on-demand delivery of knowledge. They will provide information on climate change in an interesting, interactive and experiential manner, which would enhance the learning process and increase the involvement of different stakeholders in climate action. The climate change learning lab will create widespread public awareness
